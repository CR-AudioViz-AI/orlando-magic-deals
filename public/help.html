Help Center

What is Orlando Magic Deals?

Orlando Magic Deals is a comparison platform that helps you find the best accommodation for your Disney/Orlando vacation. We compare prices across:

  • Disney Resort Hotels (direct)
  • DVC Rental options (rent points from Disney Vacation Club owners)
  • Good Neighbor Hotels (Disney-approved partner hotels)
  • Off-Site Hotels (via Expedia, Booking.com, etc.)

How do bookings work?

We are a comparison and referral service. When you click "View Deal" or "Book," you are redirected to the actual booking provider (Disney, DVC rental site, Expedia, etc.) to complete your reservation.

We never handle your payment information directly. Your booking is made with the hotel or booking platform.

What is DVC Rental?

Disney Vacation Club (DVC) members own "points" they can use to book Disney Deluxe Villa resorts. When owners can't use their points, they can rent them to non-members at a significant discount.

Example: Grand Floridian normally costs $700+/night. Via DVC rental, you might pay $350-450/night for the same room.

We partner with reputable DVC rental companies like DVC Rental Store and David's Vacation Club Rentals.
